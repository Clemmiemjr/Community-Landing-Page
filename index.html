Community

An adventure at Greendale

Check out the trailer!

A show full of whimsy, chaos and character. Community is centered around Jeff, a former lawyer forced to attend community college when it becomes clear he faked his college degree. In an attempt to seduce a cute girl from his Spanish class he forms a study group. The study group form a collection of misfits, that together create adventure, fun and ultimately...a community

Characters

Abed Nadir

Abed is an aspiring film maker, often making sense of a hectic world through the lens of films. A character known for imagination, Abed offers a relatable figure for people who struggle to relate to others. Portrayed by Danny Pudi, Abed is a fan favourite.

Troy Barnes

Troy was an aspiring athlete until a leg injury catapulted him away from the football field, and into the classroom. Portrayed by Donald Glover, Troy is Abed's accomplice in mayhem and chaos.

Jeff Winger

Jeff was a lawyer, specialising in DUI and traffic law until it was revealed the degree he held was a fake. Played by Joel McHale he's the centre of the community.

Britta Perry

Portrayed by Gillian Jacobs, is one of the core characters in Community. Despite wanting to appear virtuous, she often comes across as vain and misinformed.

Annie Edison

Driven and ambitious Annie was the founder of the "Save Greendale Committee" a campus student group. Estranged from her family, she works hard to support herself, and values the group around her.

Shirley Bennett

A mother, a business woman and a mentor, Shirley offers some much needed warmth to the study group. Played by Yvette Nicole Brown.

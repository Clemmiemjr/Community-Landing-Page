Community

An adventure at Greendale

Check out the trailer!

A show full of whimsy, chaos and character. Community is centered around Jeff, a former lawyer forced to attend community college when it becomes clear he faked his college degree. In an attempt to seduce a cute girl from his Spanish class he forms a study group. The study group form a collection of misfits, that together create adventure, fun and ultimately...a community

Abed is an aspiring film maker, often making sense of a hectic world through the lens of films. A character known for imagination, Abed offers a relatable figure for people who struggle to relate to others. Portrayed by Danny Pudi, Abed is a fan favourite.
